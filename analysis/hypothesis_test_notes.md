Hypothesis Test Notes
Business Objective
We want to check whether the new onboarding campaign actually makes a meaningful difference in improving the Paid Conversion Rate compared to the current (old) onboarding experience.
North Star Metric
Paid Conversion Rate
Null Hypothesis (H₀)
There is no real difference in Paid Conversion Rate between the Control group (old experience) and the Treatment group (new onboarding campaign).
Alternative Hypothesis (H₁)
The Treatment group has a significantly higher Paid Conversion Rate than the Control group.
Test Type
One-tailed Two-Proportion Z-Test
Significance Level
α = 0.05
Decision Rule
If the p-value < 0.05, we reject the null hypothesis and conclude that the new onboarding campaign significantly improves the Paid Conversion Rate.
If the p-value ≥ 0.05, we fail to reject the null hypothesis
